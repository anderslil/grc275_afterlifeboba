OUR STORY

Afterlife Tea And Boba Room is the only 100% vegan cafe in Las Vegas, NV, offering a wide variety of delicious options to suit every taste. We have been in business since December 2022. Our drinks are made with the finest ingredients and are always crafted to perfection. Whether you’re looking for a sweet treat for breakfast, a flavorful beverage for the afternoon, or a gift for a friend, we have the perfect drink for you.

At Afterlife Tea And Boba Room, we believe that visiting a cafe should be an experience, which is why we have created a warm and inviting atmosphere for you to enjoy. You can come in and enjoy our Lending Library, where you can take a book or leave one! Or you can take a look at our Artist Nook where local artists sell their crafts! Whether you come with friends or family, we guarantee you’ll leave feeling satisfied.

Our cafe specializes in serving coffee, milk teas, bubble teas, smoothies, fruit teas, and more! Our menu is considerate of many different palates, and you are sure to find something to enjoy on our menu. You’ll be able to taste the passion our team has for crafting drinks in every sip! At Afterlife Tea And Boba Room, we have Happy Hour (Buy One Get One 50% off) every day that we are open. Visit our boba cafe today to find out why we are the best in town!

WOMEN OWNED

We foster an inclusive and supportive environment that values diversity.

100% VEGAN

We are a 100% plant-based boba shop. We do not use any non-dairy creamers or any powders that contain milk. We take great care to ensure that our products are vegan-friendly.

LGBTQIA+ FRIENDLY

At Afterlife Tea And Boba Room, we promote inclusivity and diversity, creating an environment where everyone feels welcome and respected.

OUR MISSION

At Afterlife Tea and Boba Room, we aim not only to offer delicious boba drinks but also to support the community. We host many community events at the shop and support local artists. Without the community, we wouldn't have the success that we do, so we try to ensure that everyone feels welcomed and supported.
